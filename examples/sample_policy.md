# Acme Corp Vehicle Policy

This document outlines the vehicle policy for Acme Corp.

## Vehicle Usage
All employees must use corporate vehicles responsibly.

## Vehicle Compensation
Employees who use their personal vehicles for corporate travel are eligible for vehicle compensation.
The rate is $0.65 per mile. You must submit a log of your miles driven at the end of every month.

## Summary
Corporate vehicles are a privilege.

## Summary
Personal vehicle use is compensated appropriately.
